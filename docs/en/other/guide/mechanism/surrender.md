## {{ $frontmatter.title }}

In the game, surrender can be divided into two types:

1. Duel Surrender
2. Battle Surrender

## 1. Duel Surrender

Duel surrender triggers the following effects:

1. Duel loss
2. Duel health is restored to full

## 2. Battle Surrender

Battle surrender triggers the following effects:

1. Battle loss
